Eatery Essentials, Inc.

Eatery Essentials, Inc. is more than a manufacturer; we are a strategic partner in the global shift toward a circular economy. Headquartered in Dallas, Texas, we operate as a fully vertically integrated powerhouse, controlling every stage of production from raw rPET extrusion to precision thermoforming.
