The Koda Cup Standard

Koda Cup is Eatery Essentials' flagship line of premium disposable packaging. Designed for clarity, strength, and environmental responsibility, our products are the choice for leading cafes, restaurants, and food processors.

Unmatched Clarity

Our rPET material offers superior clarity, making your beverages and food items the star of the show. Perfect for vibrant smoothies, layered salads, and colorful parfaits.

Sustainable Design

Made with a minimum of 30% post-consumer recycled plastic, Koda Cup products reduce environmental impact without sacrificing performance or aesthetics.

Reliable Strength

Engineered for durability and spill-resistance. Precise lid fits ensure security for takeaway and delivery, preventing messy accidents.
