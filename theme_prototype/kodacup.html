The Engineering Mandate

The Koda Cup Standard

Koda Cup represents the pinnacle of vertically integrated manufacturing. By controlling every stage of production, we deliver structural integrity and clarity that the marketplace of fragmented supply chains simply cannot match.

Optical Clarity

Our rPET material is engineered for maximum light transmission, ensuring your product's natural colors and textures are the focal point. Verified clarity that rivals virgin plastic.

Precision Fit

Utilizing high-speed thermoforming with sub-millimeter tolerances, our lid-to-container seals provide industry-leading leak resistance and audible closure confirmation.

Material Strength

Strategic ribbing and wall-thickness optimization provide superior stackability and crush resistance, critical for modern high-volume fulfillment and delivery.
