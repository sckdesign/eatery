Material Science

About rPET

Sustainability has become an increasingly important consideration in the field of food packaging, as consumers and companies alike are looking for ways to reduce their environmental impact. One material that has gained popularity in recent years due to its sustainability is rPET, which is made from recycled polyethylene terephthalate (PET) plastic.

rPET is created by melting down used PET bottles and other containers, and then reforming them into new products. This process helps to conserve natural resources and reduce the amount of plastic waste that ends up in landfills. Additionally, because rPET is made from recycled materials, it generates fewer greenhouse gas emissions during its production compared to the production of new PET plastic.

Internal Innovation

Design with the Future in Mind

At Eatery Essentials, we strive to innovate with sustainability in the forefront of our designs, eliminating components that complicate the recycling stream.

Eliminating Micro-Plastics

Our tamper-evident design is a prime example of eliminating tear strips, so that our products can be completely recycled. By removing tear strips and tabs that cannot be recovered, we prevent micro-plastics from leaking into our environment.

True Recyclability

By eliminating all possible tabs that cannot be returned to the recycle stream, we ensure every part of our container has a second life. Our goal is a true circular economy.

Manufacturing Excellence

Green Manufacturing

We consider our facilities to be zero plastic waste manufacturing facilities. We achieve this by putting all scrap and unused plastic back into our manufacturing process through regrinding and re-extrusion.

Purge, floor sweepings, and other waste are sent to be chemically recycled and used in manufacturing processes downstream for food packaging.

Our facility is proud to be powered by 30% renewable resources, including solar and wind power, further reducing our operational carbon footprint.
