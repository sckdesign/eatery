The 3-Step Protocol

How Security Works

01

Press to Engage

Simply close the lid and apply light pressure to the center button. You will hear and feel the patented mechanical lock activate.

02

Secure Transit

The container is now physically locked. It resists accidental opening from drops, shifts, or external pressure during delivery.

03

Consumer Access

The end-user detaches the security tab to release the lock. This action permanently alters the container, proving first-time access.
