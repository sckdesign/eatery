Superior Engineering

Beyond Tamper-Evident

Conventional "tamper-evident" labels can be circumvented. BUT-N-LOC is an integrated architectural solution. Once the patented button is engaged, the container cannot be opened without breaking the security seal.

Mechanical Engagement

Unlike adhesive seals, our physical lock is built into the container's geometry. You get a tactile "click" that guarantees a hermetic-quality seal.

Visual Verification

The "broken button" indicator is visible from several feet away, allowing retail staff and customers to verify safety at a glance.

Structural Integrity

Heavy-duty rPET construction ensures the container maintains its shape and security even under heavy stacking in modern logistics.

The 3-Step Protocol

How Security Works

01

Press to Engage

Simply close the lid and apply light pressure to the center button. You will hear and feel the patented mechanical lock activate.

02

Secure Transit

The container is now physically locked. It resists accidental opening from drops, shifts, or external pressure during delivery.

03

Consumer Access

The end-user detaches the security tab to release the lock. This action permanently alters the container, proving first-time access.
