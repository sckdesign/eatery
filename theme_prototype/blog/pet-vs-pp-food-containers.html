PET vs PP Food Containers: Which Material is Right for Your Application?

PET vs polypropylene food containers — compare clarity, temperature resistance, recyclability, and cost to choose the right material for your food service packaging requirements.

⭐

The Verdict

For high-clarity merchandising, grab-and-go displays, and brands prioritizing established recycling streams, PET is the superior choice. Its premium look and feel drive purchasing decisions in retail environments.

However, if your operational workflow involves heat — such as hot-fill soups, microwaveable entrees, or items that must stay in warming cases — PP is the necessary choice.

At Eatery Essentials, we manufacture both. Speak with our material experts to determine which substrate fits your specific temperature and presentation requirements.
