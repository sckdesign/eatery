Eatery Essentials Launches Comprehensive 2026 Product Catalog

Eatery Essentials is proud to announce the release of our comprehensive 2026 Product Catalog, the most expansive offering in our company's history. This year's catalog reflects a decade of manufacturing expertise, customer feedback, and continued investment in our Dallas, TX production facility.

What's New in 2026

Expanded rPET Container Line

Our core rPET hinged-lid container range now spans from 4 oz. to 64 oz., with both standard and heavy-gauge wall options at every size point. All containers carry a minimum 30% post-consumer recycled content and are fully recyclable at end of life. New additions include:

  • 7" × 6" rectangle in 16 oz. and 24 oz. heavy-gauge variants
  • New vented-lid options on all round bowl sizes (18 oz. — 64 oz.)
  • Updated 2-compartment and 4-compartment 20 oz. configurations

But-N-Loc Tamper-Evident System

Our proprietary button-lock tamper-evident closure is now available on 12 additional SKUs, including the newly expanded 5.8" square bowl family in 8 oz., 12 oz., and 16 oz. sizes, each available in bulk, with dome lid combo, and flat lid combo configurations.

Koda Cup Line Updates

The Koda Cup line has been updated with improved stackability features and dimensional standardization across lid sizes. 92mm and 107mm diameter lids are now compatible across a broader range of cup sizes, reducing the number of unique lid SKUs operators need to carry.

Specialty & Snack Containers

New cake container additions — including a 6-petite format and a 10" double-layer configuration — serve the growing specialty bakery and premium dessert category. The 6" × 6" 4-compartment snack container with separate lid is designed specifically for charcuterie, cheese, and snack board applications.
