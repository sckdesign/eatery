New Production Facility in Dallas: Operations Underway

Eatery Essentials is proud to announce that our newest production facility in Dallas, TX is now fully operational. This 2-million-square-foot expansion represents a significant milestone in our commitment to domestic manufacturing and supply chain excellence.

Unmatched Logistics Advantage

Located at the crossroads of major North American shipping lanes, our Dallas facility allows us to deliver products to any major US market within days. In an era of global volatility, this domestic footprint provides our partners with a level of security that offshoring simply cannot match.

State-of-the-Art Sustainability

The facility is equipped with the latest high-efficiency thermoforming and extrusion lines, designed specifically to handle high percentages of rPET (recycled PET) content. By manufacturing close to our primary distribution hubs, we drastically reduce the carbon-miles associated with our products.
