Our Mission

A Legacy of Vertical Excellence

At Eatery Essentials, Inc., we recognize that the future of high-volume food service packaging depends on the seamless intersection of industrial scale and precision engineering. Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Dallas, Texas, we have built a reputation as North America's premier vertically integrated manufacturer of rPET and paper disposables.

Our operation is defined by a massive 2-million-square-foot facility that functions as a fully integrated ecosystem. Unlike traditional suppliers who rely on fragmented global supply chains, we control every critical milestone—from raw pigment and resin extrusion to precision thermoforming and high-speed fulfillment. This vertical model allows us to insulate our partners from market volatility, drastically reduce lead times, and maintain the industry's most rigorous quality

Our Promise

The Engineering Mandate

Sustainability is not a peripheral initiative at Eatery Essentials; it is our primary engineering mandate. Our signature rPET lines are engineered with a minimum of 30% post-consumer recycled content, diverting millions of pounds of plastic from landfills annually.

We empower our partners—from national supermarket chains to industrial processors—to hit aggressive ESG targets without ever compromising on the structural integrity or clarity of their packaging.

Manufacturing Power

State-of-the-Art Efficiency

Eatery Essentials operates at the bleeding edge of polymer science. Our Dallas facility is equipped with high-speed extrusion lines and automated thermoforming systems that operate with micron-level precision.

By managing our own regrind and re-extrusion processes, we achieve effectively zero-waste manufacturing, ensuring that every ounce of plastic within our walls is utilized for maximum value and minimal environmental impact.
