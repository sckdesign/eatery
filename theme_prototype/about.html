Our Story

Company Overview

Eatery Essentials, Inc. is focused on providing high quality, competitively priced paper and plastic food service disposables to the following segments: food service, food processors, supermarkets, C-Stores and more.

Established in 2015, Eatery Essentials is a Dallas, Texas-based company that specializes in the manufacturing of rPET disposable and recyclable food packaging. The company has quickly grown and now has distribution capabilities throughout North America.

At Eatery Essentials, we are committed to sustainability and reducing our environmental impact. Our rPET materials are made from 30% post consumer recycled plastic, making them a responsible choice for food service businesses.

Manufacturing

State-of-the-Art Facility

Eatery Essentials, Inc. is a vertically integrated extrusion and thermoforming manufacturer of polymers for drink cups, food containers, and containers with tamper evident technologies.

We conduct our operations at our new state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Dallas, TX. Our combined facilities and warehousing are in excess of 2MM sq. ft.
