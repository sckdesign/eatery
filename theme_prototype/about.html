Our Story

Company Overview

At Eatery Essentials, Inc., we believe that premium product presentation should never compromise on sustainability. Founded in 2015 and headquartered in Dallas, Texas, we have rapidly evolved into a leading North American manufacturer of high-quality, competitively priced paper and plastic food service disposables.

We are proud to operate a state-of-the-art facility spanning over 2 million square feet, functioning as a fully vertically integrated manufacturing powerhouse. This massive scale allows us to control every step of the production process—from raw resin extrusion to precision thermoforming—ensuring unrivaled quality control and rapid fulfillment for food service providers, processors, supermarkets, and C-Stores across the continent.

Sustainability is woven into the very fabric of our operations. Our signature rPET product lines are manufactured using a minimum of 30% post-consumer recycled plastic. By keeping millions of pounds of plastic out of landfills each year, we empower our partners to make responsible, eco-friendly choices without ever sacrificing structural performance or visual clarity.
